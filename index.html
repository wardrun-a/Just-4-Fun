Gender Specialist

Nataliia Koshovska is a program analyst at the United Nations Population Fund (UNFPA) in Ukraine. She has a ten-year of experience at the UN, including three years at UNFPA.

Nataliia coordinates the project "4 Hands Happiness" in cooperation with the Ministry of Social Policy of Ukraine. She also coordinates the implementation of education without discrimination and #MyCareerChoice (#професіїбезобмежень) in cooperation with the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine, as well as a project that focuses on gender inequality and domestic violence in the corporate sector.

Nataliia got a master's degree in international relations at Kyiv International University, she is a certified graduate in crisis management of Sapienza University of Rome, Italy, and a Harvard University course in Dynamic Leadership.
